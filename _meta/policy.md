# ontario-tenant-tools — Policy

## Classification
- **Default**: `public_draft`
- **Target**: `public` (Apache 2.0 open source)

## Legal Constraints
- NEVER generate legal advice
- Information tools only — calculations, timelines, glossaries
- Document generators require legal vetting before release
- Always reference source legislation (RTA 2006, Bill 60)

## Accessibility Requirements
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance (AODA requirement)
- Grade 5 reading level (plain language)
- Mobile-first, works on 3G
- High contrast (7:1 ratio)

## Privacy Requirements
- Zero data collection
- All processing in-browser
- Quick Exit button mandatory on all pages

## Dependencies
- Part of CivCitDev umbrella
- Shares approach with TenantGuard PWA
