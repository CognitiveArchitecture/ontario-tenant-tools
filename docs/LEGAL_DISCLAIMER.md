# Legal Disclaimer

## Not Legal Advice

Ontario Tenant Tools provides **general legal information** about tenant rights in Ontario, Canada. This information is educational and intended to help you understand your rights and the legal process.

**This is NOT legal advice.**

Legal advice is specific guidance about your particular situation from a qualified professional. This website cannot:

- Tell you what you should do
- Predict the outcome of your case
- Represent you at the Landlord and Tenant Board
- Create a lawyer-client relationship

## Limitations

### Information May Be Incomplete

Housing law is complex. These tools cover common situations but cannot address every circumstance. Your situation may involve factors not covered here.

### Information May Change

Laws and regulations change. While we strive to keep information current:

- Bill 60 (2025) amended many rules recently
- Some regulations are still pending
- LTB procedures may be updated
- Always verify critical deadlines with official sources

### Calculations Are Estimates

The calculators provide estimates based on information you enter. Errors in your inputs will produce incorrect results. The Landlord and Tenant Board may calculate differently.

## Where to Get Legal Help

### Free Legal Help

**Community Legal Clinics**
- Find your clinic: https://www.legalaid.on.ca/legal-clinics/
- Free services for eligible low-income Ontarians
- Many specialize in housing law

**Tenant Duty Counsel**
- Free lawyers at LTB hearings
- Available for most eviction hearings
- No income test required

**Legal Aid Ontario**
- Phone: 1-800-668-8258
- TTY: 1-866-641-8867
- https://www.legalaid.on.ca/

### Official Resources

**Landlord and Tenant Board**
- Website: https://tribunalsontario.ca/ltb/
- Phone: 1-888-332-3234
- TTY: 1-800-268-7095

**Community Legal Education Ontario (CLEO)**
- Plain-language legal information
- https://www.cleo.on.ca/
- https://stepstojustice.ca/ (guided pathways)

**Advocacy Centre for Tenants Ontario (ACTO)**
- Tenant rights resources
- https://www.acto.ca/
